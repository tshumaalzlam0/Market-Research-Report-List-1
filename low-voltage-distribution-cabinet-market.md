<p><p>Low Voltage Distribution Cabinets are categorized into three main types: stationary, drawer, and frame. </p><p>Stationary cabinets are fixed enclosures designed for housing electrical components and protecting them from external elements, suitable for permanent installations. Drawer cabinets feature removable units that allow easy access to components for maintenance, making them ideal for environments requiring frequent servicing. Frame cabinets are open structures that support modular systems, providing flexibility for various configurations and future expansions, often used in larger industrial applications where adaptability is essential.</p></p>
