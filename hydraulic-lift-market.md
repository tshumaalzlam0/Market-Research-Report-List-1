Aerial lifts provide a platform that can be raised to various heights, suitable for tasks requiring greater reach. Rotary lifts use hydraulic systems to elevate vehicles, commonly utilized in auto repair and maintenance, showcasing the versatility and applications within the hydraulic lift market.</p></p>
