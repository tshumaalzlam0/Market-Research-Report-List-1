<p><ul><li>Sheet-based Membrane</li><li>Liquid Applied Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The post-applied waterproofing membrane market is divided into two primary types: sheet-based membranes and liquid-applied membranes. Sheet-based membranes consist of pre-formed, durable materials that are rolled out and adhered to surfaces, providing robust protection against water infiltration. In contrast, liquid-applied membranes are applied as a liquid coating that cures to form a seamless, flexible layer, allowing for easy application over complex shapes and details. Both types offer effective waterproofing solutions for various construction and infrastructure projects.</p></p>
