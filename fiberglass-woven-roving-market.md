<p><ul><li>Single-end Roving</li><li>Multi-end Roving</li><li>Chopped Roving</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiberglass woven roving market comprises three primary types: single-end roving, multi-end roving, and chopped roving. Single-end roving consists of continuous strands of glass fibers, providing strength and versatility for applications like boat manufacturing and automotive parts. Multi-end roving features multiple strands bundled together, enhancing strength for large composite structures. Chopped roving, consisting of short fiber lengths, is used primarily in applications requiring easy mixing and molding, such as in the production of fiberglass reinforced plastics.</p></p>
