<p><p>Commercial rubber flooring is a durable, resilient option for high-traffic areas, available in natural and synthetic rubber types. Natural rubber flooring, derived from rubber tree latex, offers eco-friendliness, excellent elasticity, and slip resistance, making it ideal for gyms and healthcare facilities. Synthetic rubber flooring, made from petroleum-based materials like SBR or NBR, provides superior durability, chemical resistance, and cost-effectiveness, suitable for industrial and commercial spaces. Both types are low-maintenance, noise-reducing, and customizable, catering to diverse functional and aesthetic needs in the market.</p></p>
