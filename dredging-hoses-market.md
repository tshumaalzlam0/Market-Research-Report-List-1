<p><p>The dredging hoses market is primarily segmented into two types: floating dredging hoses and non-floating dredging hoses. Floating dredging hoses are designed to remain on the surface of the water, facilitating the transport of sediments and other materials while minimizing drag and enhancing maneuverability during dredging operations. In contrast, non-floating dredging hoses are typically submerged, providing stability and efficiency in deeper water applications. Both types are essential for effective dredging operations, catering to different environmental and operational needs.</p></p>
