<p><ul><li>Pitched Roof</li><li>Flat Roof</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric skylight market is primarily divided into two types based on roof design: pitched roof and flat roof. Pitched roof skylights are angled, designed to enhance natural light and ventilation in sloped ceilings, making them popular in residential homes. In contrast, flat roof skylights are integrated into horizontal surfaces, ideal for modern architecture, allowing for easy installation and maximizing daylight in commercial and residential buildings. Both types enhance energy efficiency and aesthetics while providing a seamless connection to the outdoors.</p></p>
