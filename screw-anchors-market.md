<p><p>The screw anchors market is primarily categorized into stud anchors and sleeved anchors. Stud anchors typically feature a long threaded rod, designed for direct embedding into materials like concrete, providing strong, reliable tension loads. In contrast, sleeved anchors come with an expansion mechanism housed in a sleeve, allowing for easy installation into various base materials while offering flexibility in load capacities. Both types serve essential functions in construction, providing secure fastening solutions for various applications.</p></p>
