<p><p>The sand washing equipment market encompasses various types, primarily including spiral sand washers and wheel sand washers. Spiral sand washers utilize a helical design to separate finer particles from coarser sand, providing efficient cleaning. Wheel sand washers, on the other hand, employ a circular motion to effectively remove impurities and aggregate grains. Additionally, the "other" market segment includes alternative technologies like vibratory and bucket-type washing systems, offering varied solutions for sand processing based on specific requirements and applications.</p></p>
