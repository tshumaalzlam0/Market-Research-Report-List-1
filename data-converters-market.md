<p><p>The data converters market primarily consists of Analog-to-Digital Converters (ADCs) and Digital-to-Analog Converters (DACs). ADCs transform analog signals into digital data, enabling digital processing and storage, crucial in applications like telecommunications and healthcare. Conversely, DACs convert digital signals back into analog form, allowing devices to produce sound or control analog systems. Both types are essential in various sectors, including consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial automation, facilitating seamless communication between the analog and digital worlds.</p></p>
