<p><p>The Minimum Quantity Lubrication (MQL) system market encompasses various lubrication types. Oil lubrication systems utilize liquid oil to reduce friction, while grease lubrication systems employ thicker lubricants suitable for slower applications. Recirculating oil lubrication systems continuously circulate oil for consistent lubrication. Air/oil lubrication systems mix air with oil for effective application in high-speed machines. Near dry machining (MQL) focuses on using minimal lubrication, enhancing efficiency and reducing waste in machining processes, promoting sustainability and cost-effectiveness in manufacturing.</p></p>
