<p><p>The diaphragm for vanadium cell market includes various types of membranes that facilitate ion exchange processes critical for vanadium redox flow batteries. Cation exchange membranes selectively allow positive ions to pass, enhancing efficiency in the electrochemical reaction. Anion exchange membranes, conversely, permit negative ions to move, supporting overall battery balance. Amphoteric ion exchange membranes combine properties of both, accommodating diverse ions and improving energy storage performance. Each type plays a crucial role in optimizing battery function and longevity.</p></p>
