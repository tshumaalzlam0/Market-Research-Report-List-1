<p><p>The laser barcode scanner market is divided into two main types: portable barcode scanners and fixed mount scanners. Portable barcode scanners are handheld devices, providing flexibility and convenience for mobile scanning in retail, warehouses, and logistics. They allow operators to scan items on-the-go. In contrast, fixed mount scanners are stationary units, integrated into checkout counters or conveyor systems, offering high-speed scanning for high-volume environments. Both types enhance efficiency and accuracy in inventory management and transaction processing.</p></p>
