<p><p>The plant-based humectants market encompasses diverse categories including sugar alcohols, glycerol, alpha hydroxy acids, polysaccharides, glycols, and others. Glycols, often used in cosmetic formulations, serve as effective humectants, keeping products smooth and preventing moisture loss.</p></p>
