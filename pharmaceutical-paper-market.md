<p><p>The Pharmaceutical Paper Market includes various types based on weight, measured in grams per square meter (gsm). **40gsm** paper is lightweight, often used for primary packaging like labels and inserts. **45gsm** offers slightly more durability, suitable for blister packaging and intermediate wrapping. **50gsm** is thicker, ideal for secondary packaging and ensuring better protection. The **Other** category includes papers above 50gsm, used for specialized applications requiring higher strength or barrier properties, such as tamper-proof packaging or moisture-resistant solutions. Each type caters to specific pharmaceutical packaging needs.</p></p>
