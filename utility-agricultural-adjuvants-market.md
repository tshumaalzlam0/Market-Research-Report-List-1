<p><p>Utility agricultural adjuvants enhance the effectiveness of pesticides and fertilizers by improving their performance and application. Compatibility agents ensure that multiple products can mix safely without degrading, while buffers and acidifiers adjust the pH of sprays for optimal efficacy. Antifoam agents minimize foam during mixing and application, and water conditioners eliminate harmful impurities in water, enhancing solution stability. Anti-drift agents reduce spray drift during application, ensuring targeted delivery, while other utility adjuvants provide additional functionalities specific to user needs.</p></p>
