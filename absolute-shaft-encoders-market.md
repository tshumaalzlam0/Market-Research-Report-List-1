<p><p>The absolute shaft encoders market is divided into two main types: single-turn and multi-turn encoders. Single-turn encoders provide a unique position reading within a single rotation, typically offering high resolution for applications requiring precise angular measurement. In contrast, multi-turn encoders track rotational positions over multiple turns, retaining positional data even when power is lost, making them ideal for applications needing extensive rotation tracking. Each type serves distinct applications based on precision and rotational range requirements.</p></p>
