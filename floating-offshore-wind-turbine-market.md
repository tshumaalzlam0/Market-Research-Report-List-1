<p><strong>The Floating Offshore Wind Turbine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Axis Wind Turbines</li><li>Vertical Axis Wind Turbines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The floating offshore wind turbine market comprises two main types: horizontal axis wind turbines (HAWTs) and vertical axis wind turbines (VAWTs). HAWTs, characterized by their blades rotating around a horizontal shaft, are widely used due to their efficiency and high energy output. In contrast, VAWTs have blades that rotate around a vertical axis, offering advantages such as lower maintenance costs and capability to capture wind from any direction. Both types are pivotal for harnessing wind energy in deeper waters.</p></p>
